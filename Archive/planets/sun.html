      Did you know?
      • Our Sun is a 4.5 billion-year-old star.
      • Our Sun is made of hydrogen and helium at the centre of our solar system.
      • The Sun is about 93 million miles (150 million kilometers) from Earth
      • The Sun’s volume would need 1.3 million Earths to fill it
      • The hottest part of the Sun is its core, where temperatures top 15 million degrees Celsius
      • The Sun doesn’t have moons, but it’s orbited by eight planets, at least five dwarf planets, tens of thousands of asteroids, and perhaps three trillion comets and icy bodies.
